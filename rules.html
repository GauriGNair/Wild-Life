Wildlife Park Regulations and Rules

To protect the animals and maintain the natural environment, visitors must follow the park rules carefully. These regulations help ensure the safety of both visitors and wildlife.

Visitor Guidelines

  • Do not feed the animals. Human food can harm them.
  • Do not litter inside the park. Use the dustbins provided.
  • Maintain silence while observing wildlife.
  • Always follow the instructions of park guides and staff.
  • Do not enter restricted areas.
  • Avoid using flash photography as it can disturb animals.
  • Respect the natural habitat and avoid damaging plants.

Following these rules helps preserve wildlife and ensures that future visitors can also enjoy the beauty of nature.
